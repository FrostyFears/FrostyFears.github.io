Meeting Your Needs

Data Cleaning

Whether it be validation, missingness or handling free text, I have experience in both data wrangling and management from project start to finish. I am experienced in handling data from standard sequencing datasets to more variable EHR datasets.

Statistical Analysis

My PhD work was as part of a statistical genetics lab in which I worked on method development and statistical consulting work. I experience in applying statistical methods from simple power caluclations to deploying advanced machine learning methods such as elastic net, xgboost and neural networks.

Pipeline Management

Taking care to manage an experiment from start to finish can be a monumentous task with many intermediate steps necessary for following propper proceedures. From RNA-seq analysis to metagenomics analysis, I can ensure your pipelines are scalable and reproducable.

Genomic Analyses

Bioinformatics and genomics is a large umbrella containing many types of analysis. I am familiar with, and have experience in many such analysis, including genome assembly, variant calling, functional genetics, GWAS, meta-analysis and comparative genomics.

As the field continues to grow, you need experts with a proven trackrecord of understanding, handling and staying up-to-date as new analyses emerge.
